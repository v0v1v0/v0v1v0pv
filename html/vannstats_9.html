howell_aids_wide R Documentation

Howell Student AIDS Knowledge Data (Wide Form)

Description

This data set, from Howell, measures students' knowledge at three time points, in wide form.

Usage

howell_aids_wide

Format

A data frame with 4 observations and 4 variables.

student student id
t1 student AIDS knowledge score at time 1
t2 student AIDS knowledge score at time 2
t3 student AIDS knowledge score at time 3
