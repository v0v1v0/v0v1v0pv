GSS2014 R Documentation

General Social Survey, 2014

Description

This subset of data comes from one iteration of the General Social Survey, administered in 2014. These data were collected by the National Opinion Research Center (NORC) at the University of Chicago. The observations represent individuals' responses to survey questions. Information about the data set can be found in the GSS Codebook at: https://burrelvannjr.com/docs/GSS_Codebook.pdf.

Usage
