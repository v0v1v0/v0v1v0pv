pac.clas.Nadults R Documentation

Harmonic mean of adults and population sizes

Description

Data from Pacioni et al. (2017) - sensitivity test scenario 'ST_Classic' - were used to calculate the harmonic mean of adults and population sizes using Nadults.

Format

A data.frame with 24 observations of 4 variables.

Source

Pacioni, C., Williams, M., Lacy RC, Spencer, P.B.S. and Wayne, A.F. (2017) Predators and genetic fitness: key threatening factors for the conservation of bettong species. Pacific Conservation Biology. DOI:10.1071/PC17002

Examples

data("pac.clas.Nadults")
head(pac.clas.Nadults)
