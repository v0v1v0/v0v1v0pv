wffc.teams R Documentation

2008 World Fly Fishing Championships (Team results) Data

Description

Team results of the 2008 FIPS-MOUCHE World Fly Fishing Championships held in Rotorua, New Zealand during 22â€“30 March 2008.

Usage

data(wffc.teams)

Format

A data frame with 18 observations on the following 5 variables. Some of these variable are described in wffc.

country

a character vector.

totalPlacings

a numeric vector; these are the summed placings over the 5 sessions and 5 team members.

points

a numeric vector; see wffc.

noofcaptures

a numeric vector.

longestfish

a numeric vector.

Details

This data frame gives the team results of the competition. See also wffc and wffc.indiv for more details and links.

Examples
