sta.main R Documentation

Collated results from Vortex scenarios - Campbell et al (2016)

Description

A dataset with the results from the main Vortex scenarios used in Campbell et al (2016). Vortex outputs, from the project named 'Starlingv3PopBased' (.dat files), were collated with collate_dat.

Format

a data.frame with 1632 observations of 44 variables.

Source

Campbell et al. (2016). Assessing the economic benefits of starling detection and control to Western Australia. Australasian Journal of Environmental Management, 23, 81-99 DOI:10.1080/14486563.2015.1028486

Examples

data("sta.main")
